Hotel Xenia FAQ's

My flight arrives early in the morning, what time can I check in?
Standard check-in time is 2:00 PM. However, we can accommodate early check-ins subject to room availability. Please contact the front desk in advance.
Is breakfast included as standard with all rooms?
Yes, a complimentary buffet breakfast is included with all room bookings, served at our main restaurant from 7:00 AM to 10:30 AM.
Can I smoke in the hotel rooms?
All our rooms are non-smoking to ensure a fresh environment for all guests. We have designated smoking areas available within the hotel premises.
Are airport pickups available for late night flight arrivals?
Yes, we offer 24/7 airport pickup and drop-off services. You can arrange this via our concierge or by selecting the option during booking.
